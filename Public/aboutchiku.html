About Chiku

Chiranjib Sonowal is a passionate traveler and cultural explorer hailing from Dibrugarh, Assam. He serves as the Human Resources and Operations Manager at Oddessemania Private Limited, a travel startup recognized as the Best Travel Startup in Northeast India. This platform offers guide-aided travel experiences, connecting travelers with local, trained guides to provide immersive journeys.

Beyond his professional role, Chiranjib founded Uncharted Voyage, a platform dedicated to sharing unique travel stories and experiences. He is also a founded Uncharted Weg, a community of philanthropists nurturing future generations by distributing knowledge in rural areas, reflecting his diverse interests.

His personal Instagram account, @wanderwith_chiku, showcases his adventures, poetry, and explorations, emphasizing his deep connection to travel and culture.

Chiranjib's commitment to promoting Northeast India's rich heritage is evident through his active engagement in various cultural and travel initiatives.
